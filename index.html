About the Club

The Waxhaw Italian Club brings neighbors together to share language, food, music, and traditions—from espresso tastings to film nights and regional cooking workshops. Whether your roots are in Italy or you simply love the culture, you’re welcome here.

  • Monthly cultural meetups and guest speakers
  • Conversation tables (all levels welcome)
  • Community service and local partnerships in Waxhaw
