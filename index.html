About the Club

The Waxhaw Italian Club brings neighbors together to share language, food, music, and traditions—from espresso tastings to film nights and regional cooking workshops. Whether your roots are in Sicily, Calabria, Piemonte, or you're simply Italiano nel cuore, you’re welcome here.

  • Monthly cultural meetups and guest speakers
  • Italian conversation tables (all levels welcome)
  • Community service & local partnerships in Waxhaw
